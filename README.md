Some custom content for a DnD 5e campaign i wanted to put inside Aurora Builder

Put this link in Aurora Bulder additional content tab

https://raw.githubusercontent.com/esmer-1/Talents-and-stuff/refs/heads/main/laurence-stuff.index

1. How to get custom talents to show up
equipment->talents->->add the talent to inventory and click "equip"

2. How to get bonus hp we got at lvl 1
equipment->Wondrous Item->filter sources for "Laurence Stuff"->add the hp you got from the roll and click "equip",  you can also click on "details" of health item and uncheck all three options at the bottom so it won't show in equipment section of character sheet

3. One more thing i did was adding new skills (spellpower, society, concentration).
They show up in new category of equipment "New Skills", they show up on racial traits of the aurora character sheet (didn't want to put more things in features and traits"
How to get them
equipment->New Skills->pick your skill level (no proficiency, proficiency, expertise)->add the skill to inventory and click "equip"
be mindful that there is a choice of skill for spellpower, pick the one your class uses
